Hortifruti E-commerce

Mobile application made with Flutter and Dart during mobile development. Developed an e-commerce for the fruit and vegetable sector that includes products listings and a shopping cart.

GITHUB

Project overview

Status: Under development.

Project developed during the Programming classes for Mobile Devices II, taught by Professor Rogério de Andrade, during the 5th semester of the Development of Multiplataform Software, at FATEC in São José dos Campos - Jessen Vidal.

The project consists of developing an e-commerce store with a shopping cart. The application login, a list of products and the possibility of adding products to a shopping cart and placing an order were developed. Several tools provided by the language choosed were used. Even with approval of the project in class, the development and improvement of this project are still active.
